> Source: http://www.pnotepad.org/docs/navigation/keyboard_shortcuts/

> Aliases: notepad-editor, notepad-text-editor

$ Bookmarks
    `Ctrl Space                    {{Next bookmark}} 

$ File
    `Ctrl N                        {{New file}} 
    `Ctrl O                        {{Open file}} 
    `Ctrl P                        {{Print file}} 
    `Ctrl S                        {{Save file}} 

$ Edit
    `Ctrl Z                        {{Undo}} 
    `Ctrl Y                        {{Redo}} 
    `Ctrl X                        {{Cut}} 
    `Ctrl V                        {{Paste}} 
    `Ctrl C                        {{Copy}}
