# YouTube

> Source: http://www.hongkiat.com/blog/youtube-keyboard-shortcuts/

> Aliases: you-tube

$ Common Shortcuts
    `K                             {{Play / Pause}} 
    `M                             {{Mute}} 
    `J                             {{Go back 10 seconds}} 
    `L                             {{Go forward 10 seconds}} 
    `←                             {{Go back 5 seconds}} 
    `→                             {{Go forward 5 seconds}} 
    `↑                             {{Increase Volume}} 
    `↓                             {{Decrease Volume}} 
    `F                             {{Fullscreen}} 
    `Esc                           {{Exit Fullscreen}} 

$ Skip to certain parts of the video
    `0                             {{Go to the 0% mark}} 
    `1                             {{Go to the 10% mark}} 
    `2                             {{Go to the 20% mark}} 
    `3                             {{Go to the 30% mark}} 
    `4                             {{Go to the 40% mark}} 
    `5                             {{Go to the 50% mark}} 
    `6                             {{Go to the 60% mark}} 
    `7                             {{Go to the 70% mark}} 
    `8                             {{Go to the 80% mark}} 
    `9                             {{Go to the 90% mark}} 
    `End                           {{Go to the End}}
